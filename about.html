Heyo, I'm Amal - a designer, developer, educator and researcher hoping to further my career in the UX design space.

I discovered a love for tech and art during high school in the Computer Lab and Art Hall. I graduated from King Abdulaziz University, with a B.A. in European languages with a Focus in English language and literature.

Before shifting to UX design, I worked as an English instructor to diploma students. But after a couple of years in the education field, I felt restless and wanted to do more. So, I started to experiment with different things like data analysis, python programming and web development.
During this phase, I went through personal struggles that made me fascinated with the way people think, feel, and behave. All of that gave me a sense of direction towards ux design where art, tech and psychology are combined.

When I am not working or reading, I love to play with oil paints, take interesting shots with my camera and explore the outdoors.

I also like collaborating and chatting over a good cup of coffee, so get in touch or recommend a coffee shop.
